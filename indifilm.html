INTRODUCTION

‘Indifilm’ allows people to stream and download movies across multiple devices. Indifilm uses a subscription basis wherein each person creates an account and stores these movies on the cloud.

During the launch of Netflix India, in 2016, one of my friends was telling me about how it could revolutionize the digital industry - and how it would kill off standard DTH operators and cable TV operators across the country.

It is now 2017, and I cannot wholeheartedly agree with his statement. This is due to a variety of reasons:

  1. The prices of streaming services are too expensive for a third world country like India

  2. There isn’t a lot of content available, primarily due to censorship by the CBFC

  3. Weak content recommendation

Based on these three shortcomings, I decided to find a solution - in the form of a mobile application which could rival the services offered by Netflix. This solution is “Indifilm”.
